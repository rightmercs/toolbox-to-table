# The Inspector's Handbook Series

Built for the field. Written for the professional.

The Inspector's Handbook Series is a growing library of practical field guides developed to support inspectors, welders, fitters, QA/QC personnel, supervisors, technicians, and industrial professionals. Each guide focuses on real-world application, workflow awareness, lessons learned, inspection practices, and quick-reference information that can be used in the field.

Whether you're performing inspections, managing quality activities, overseeing fabrication, supporting construction, or preparing for the next turnaround, these pocket-sized references are designed to help you work more efficiently, make better decisions, and improve overall project execution.

## Complete Series

18 Pocket-Sized Field Guides Designed to Support Industrial Professionals Through Real-World Application, Workflow Awareness, Lessons Learned, and Practical Reference Materials.
