# The Inspector's Handbook Series

Built for the field. Written for the inspector.

The Inspector's Handbook Series provides practical guidance, workflows, checklists, lessons learned, and quick-reference information for industrial professionals working in fabrication, construction, maintenance, inspection, testing, and quality disciplines.
