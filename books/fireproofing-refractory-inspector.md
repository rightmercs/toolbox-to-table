# The Fireproofing & Refractory Inspector

![Fireproofing & Refractory Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/fireproofing-refractory-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Fireproofing & Refractory Inspector provides practical guidance for professionals responsible for inspecting passive fire protection systems and refractory installations. Covering material verification, substrate preparation, installation activities, curing requirements, testing considerations, and documentation review, the guide helps inspectors understand critical quality checkpoints throughout the process.

## Key Topics Covered

* Material verification
* Surface preparation
* Fireproofing installation
* Refractory installation
* Curing requirements
* Quality verification
* Documentation review
* Final inspection activities
