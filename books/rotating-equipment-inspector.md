# The Rotating Equipment Inspector

![Rotating Equipment Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/rotating-equipment-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Rotating Equipment Inspector provides practical guidance for inspectors and quality professionals involved in the manufacture, installation, inspection, and maintenance of rotating machinery. Covering equipment verification, dimensional inspection, assembly activities, testing requirements, documentation review, and turnover processes, the guide helps inspectors understand critical quality checkpoints while supporting successful equipment performance and reliability.

## Key Topics Covered

* Equipment verification
* Dimensional inspection
* Assembly oversight
* Alignment awareness
* Testing requirements
* Documentation review
* Turnover activities
* Reliability considerations
