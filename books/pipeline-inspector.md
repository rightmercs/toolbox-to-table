# The Pipeline Inspector

![Pipeline Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/pipeline-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Pipeline Inspector provides practical field guidance for professionals involved in pipeline construction, fabrication, installation, testing, and turnover activities. Covering inspection planning, material verification, welding operations, coating activities, hydrotesting, and final documentation, the guide follows the typical lifecycle of a pipeline project. Lessons learned, inspection workflows, and quick-reference materials help inspectors identify concerns early and improve overall project execution while maintaining compliance with applicable requirements.

## Key Topics Covered

* Pipeline construction workflow
* Material traceability
* Welding inspection
* Coating inspection
* Hydrotesting
* Right-of-way activities
* Documentation review
* Turnover requirements
