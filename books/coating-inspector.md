# The Coating Inspector

![Coating Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/coating-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Coating Inspector provides practical field guidance for inspectors involved in surface preparation, coating application, curing, testing, and documentation activities. The guide follows the typical workflow encountered during coating projects and emphasizes inspection techniques, environmental monitoring, surface profile verification, dry film thickness measurement, and final acceptance activities.

## Key Topics Covered

* Surface preparation
* Environmental monitoring
* Surface profile inspection
* Coating application
* Dry film thickness measurement
* Holiday testing
* Documentation review
* Final acceptance activities
