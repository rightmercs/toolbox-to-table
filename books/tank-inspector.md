# The Tank Inspector

![Tank Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/tank-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Tank Inspector provides practical guidance for inspectors involved in the fabrication, erection, testing, and documentation of storage tanks and related equipment. The guide covers material verification, fit-up activities, welding oversight, dimensional checks, testing requirements, and final turnover processes. Designed as a field reference, it follows the typical inspection workflow encountered during tank construction projects.

## Key Topics Covered

* Tank fabrication
* Material verification
* Fit-up inspection
* Welding oversight
* Dimensional verification
* Testing requirements
* Documentation review
* Turnover activities
