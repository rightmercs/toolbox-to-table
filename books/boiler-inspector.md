# The Boiler Inspector

![Boiler Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/boiler-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Boiler Inspector provides practical field guidance for inspectors involved in the fabrication, repair, alteration, and testing of boiler systems and pressure-retaining components. Covering inspection planning, welding oversight, material verification, pressure testing, and documentation requirements, the guide follows the typical workflow encountered during boiler projects. Practical checklists, lessons learned, and quick-reference information help inspectors identify concerns, maintain compliance, and improve inspection effectiveness. Designed as a portable reference, it supports both experienced inspectors and those entering boiler-related inspection activities.

## Key Topics Covered

* Boiler inspection workflow
* Material verification
* Welding oversight
* Dimensional inspection
* Pressure testing
* Documentation requirements
* Repair and alteration considerations
* Final inspection activities
