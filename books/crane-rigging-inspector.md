# The Crane & Rigging Inspector

![Crane & Rigging Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/crane-rigging-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Crane & Rigging Inspector provides practical guidance for professionals responsible for inspecting lifting equipment, rigging systems, and lifting operations. The guide covers inspection planning, equipment verification, rigging awareness, documentation requirements, and field oversight activities. Practical lessons learned and field references help inspectors identify potential concerns before they become incidents.

## Key Topics Covered

* Crane inspection
* Rigging inspection
* Lift planning awareness
* Equipment verification
* Documentation requirements
* Field oversight
* Safety considerations
* Inspection workflow
