# The Piping Inspector

![Piping Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/piping-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Piping Inspector provides practical guidance for piping fabrication, installation, testing, and inspection activities encountered throughout industrial construction projects. Covering everything from material receipt and fabrication through hydrotesting and final turnover, the guide follows the typical workflow experienced by field inspectors and quality professionals. Practical inspection checklists, lessons learned, and quick-reference materials help identify issues early while improving consistency and efficiency during project execution. Designed as a pocket-sized field companion, this guide helps professionals navigate piping activities with confidence.

## Key Topics Covered

* Material receiving and traceability
* Pipe fabrication and fit-up
* Welding inspection
* Dimensional verification
* Hydrotesting
* Documentation requirements
* Turnover activities
* Inspection workflow
