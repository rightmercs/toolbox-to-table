# The Structural Steel Inspector

![Structural Steel Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/structural-steel-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Structural Steel Inspector provides practical guidance for inspectors and quality professionals involved in the fabrication, erection, and verification of structural steel systems. The guide covers material verification, fit-up inspection, welding oversight, bolting activities, dimensional checks, coating considerations, and documentation requirements. Following the typical workflow encountered during structural steel projects, it provides practical lessons learned, inspection checklists, and quick-reference information that help improve consistency and quality throughout fabrication and construction activities.

## Key Topics Covered

* Structural steel fabrication
* Material verification
* Fit-up inspection
* Welding oversight
* Bolting inspection
* Dimensional verification
* Coating awareness
* Documentation requirements
