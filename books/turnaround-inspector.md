# The Turnaround Inspector

![Turnaround Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/turnaround-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Turnaround Inspector is designed to support inspectors and quality personnel working during plant outages, shutdowns, and turnaround events. The guide focuses on planning, execution, documentation, communication, and inspection activities that occur under compressed schedules and high work volumes. Practical checklists, field lessons, and workflow guidance help inspectors manage competing priorities while maintaining quality and safety expectations throughout turnaround execution.

## Key Topics Covered

* Turnaround planning
* Inspection scheduling
* Work package review
* Quality hold points
* Documentation control
* Communication practices
* Punch list management
* Turnover activities
