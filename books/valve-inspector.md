# The Valve Inspector

![Valve Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/valve-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Valve Inspector provides practical guidance for inspectors involved in the manufacture, assembly, testing, inspection, and documentation of industrial valves. Covering material verification, dimensional inspection, assembly activities, pressure testing, documentation review, and turnover requirements, the guide follows the typical workflow encountered during valve manufacturing and quality verification activities.

## Key Topics Covered

* Material verification
* Valve assembly
* Dimensional inspection
* Pressure testing
* Documentation review
* Quality hold points
* Turnover activities
* Inspection workflow
