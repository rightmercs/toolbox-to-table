# The Heat Exchanger Inspector

![Heat Exchanger Inspector Cover](/toolbox-to-table/assets/book-covers/heat-exchanger-inspector-cover.jpg)

## Description

The Heat Exchanger Inspector is designed to support inspectors and quality personnel involved in the fabrication, assembly, testing, and documentation of heat transfer equipment. The guide covers inspection activities related to shell fabrication, tube installation, welding operations, dimensional verification, testing requirements, and turnover documentation. Practical workflows and field-focused guidance help inspectors understand critical quality checkpoints while maintaining compliance throughout the manufacturing process. Lessons learned and quick-reference information make this guide an effective field resource for both new and experienced professionals.

## Key Topics Covered

* Heat exchanger fabrication
* Tube bundle inspection
* Welding activities
* Dimensional verification
* Testing requirements
* Documentation review
* Quality hold points
* Turnover activities
